    A large number of people today depend on delivery services for their day-to-day business or leisure. The reasons are multiple. Some of them being, a busy lifestyle, lack of time and patience to waste in Lagos traffic or avoiding stress, etc. When you are busy in the office and needs to get something done or deliver and you have no where else to look, this is the problem Easydispatch set out to solve. And I am challenged to design a better for its potential users.

  • EasyDispatch want to developed a better logistics experience that people can use to get things done swiftly and seamlessly.
