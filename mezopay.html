  • MEZOPAY

    Mezopay is a consumers payment application developed for the sole purpose of helping manage money and checkout fast from any store they go to. Currently there are no digital app that let you shop or buy with ease of mind, everyone still uses card to run transaction. Mezopay want to improve the experience of how people shop both online and in real live.

  • Mezopay is a subsidiary of Mezovest who is currently solving problems for businesses. Mezovest want to help business process day to day transactions with Mezopay, give individuals and businesses a platform to connect and transact — Mezopay want to be bridge the gap for consumer to transact with businesses. Mezopay is design to aid seamless transaction for individual to buy from their favourite vendors conveniently.
